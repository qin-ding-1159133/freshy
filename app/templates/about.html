About Fresh Harvest Delivery

Fresh Harvest Delivery is your go-to online company for fresh and delicious produce. We specialize in delivering farm-fresh fruits, crisp vegetables, fragrant herbs, and more directly to your doorstep. Our mission is to bring the best of the harvest to your home, ensuring you enjoy the highest quality and freshest produce available.

Our Story

Established to bridge the gap between local farms and urban homes, Fresh Harvest Delivery started with a simple idea: to make fresh, healthy produce accessible and convenient for everyone. Over time, we've expanded our operations to include five depots across Christchurch, Invercargill, Wellington, Hamilton, and Auckland, delivering within the local metropolitan areas.

Our dedicated team, including all the staff, local managers, and a national manager, works tirelessly to ensure that your orders are handled with care and delivered on time. We believe in supporting local farmers and providing our customers with the freshest seasonal produce available.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience for our customers. We aim to:

  • Deliver the freshest and highest quality produce directly to your door.
  • Offer a wide variety of fruits, vegetables, herbs, and more, sourced from local farms.
  • Provide flexible ordering options, including individual items and premade boxes.
  • Ensure convenience with subscription options for regular deliveries.
  • Maintain a user-friendly platform for easy shopping and efficient order management.

Our Products

We offer a diverse range of products, including:

  • Fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Herbs and salads
  • Eggs and honey
  • Premade boxes in various sizes (small, medium, large) for one-time or subscription-based orders

Depending on the item, you can order by units of specified bunches, weights (generally 500g or 1kg), each, half size, punnet, or tray. Our premade boxes can be customized to fit your lifestyle and dietary preferences, and are available for weekly, fortnightly, or monthly delivery.

Our Commitment to Quality

At Fresh Harvest Delivery, quality is at the heart of everything we do. We carefully select our produce from trusted local farmers to ensure that only the best reaches your table. Our team is committed to providing exceptional customer service and making sure your shopping experience is seamless and satisfying.
